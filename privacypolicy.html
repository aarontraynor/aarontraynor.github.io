Cookies

This website uses cookies to determine when someone is loading the webpage for the first time. By using cookies in this way, the website can know when it needs to load certain content, such as the cookies warning that led you to this page!

Once a user has accepted that the site uses cookies, they no longer need to see the message informing them about the use of cookies on this site - it just gets in the way! Only displaying this message when the user has not yet accepted the use of cookies improves the experience of using the site while ensuring the site complies with EU legislation on cookies.

This site also uses cookies as part of Google Analytics, which you can read more about in the section below.

Still confused about what cookies are used for, or just want to learn more about them? Visit this website, or learn more about the EU legislation on cookies.

Google Analytics

Google Analytics is a service provided by Google that anonymously collects data about the users visiting a given website. It enables the owner of a site to better understand their audience and see what pages of their site are viewed the most. It provides information such as geographical location (country and city only), device and browser used, time spent on the site, pages visited and how the user reached the site. When using certain browsers that are connected to a Google account, visits from different devices may be grouped to better identify unique visits, even when made across multiple devices.

Please note that the data received from Google Anaylitics is always anonymous; at no point does Google provide data that is personally identifiable to the site owner. However, it is important to note that Google may be able to identify users through the data it collects, especially if your browser is connected to a Google account.

If you do not wish to participate in Google Analytics, you can install this browser extension provided by Google to opt-out.

This site does not use Google Analytics' Advertising Features. For more information about how Google uses your data, visit their site.
